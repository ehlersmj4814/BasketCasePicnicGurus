Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide alcohol?

NO. We do not have proper licensing, and not all locations permit alcohol. We do have packages that include juices/mixers and barware. Alcohol is at your discretion.

Will you come to any location?

We will book picnics anywhere within approximately 45 minutes from Phoenix. Please contact us if you would like to book at a custom or further location.

What locations do you recommend?

Our locations are listed on the page following the package selection. Popular locations include Tempe Town Lake, Fountain Hills Park, and Papago Park.

What is your cancellation policy?

There are no refunds for cancellations, as we have already spent time and money on ordering and picking up food and planning for your picnic. If you need to reschedule more than 2 weeks from your booking, we can reschedule for a future date within one year from the original booking date at no extra charge.

What is your inclement weather policy?

In the case of inclement weather, we can plan for an alternative location, which could potentially include parks with gazebos or pavilions, or even your backyard or living room!

How many people can I bring on a picnic?

The listed picnic package prices are for two attendees. We can do picnics for up to 8 people. The pricing will follow the base price listed for two people.

Can I bring my own decorations or food?

You may bring your own gifts, cards, photos, or other sentimental items. We ask that you allow us to provide all other decorations and food. We have many add-on and customization options to create your perfect outing!
